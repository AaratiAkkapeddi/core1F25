Digital Collage

First, read Mindy Seuâ€™s article in Distributed Web of Care. Create a one-page website that functions as a mini-archive, a digital collage that preserves and reframes a collection of images and words. Like the online archives described in Distributed Web of Care, your project should act as a way of holding, arranging, and giving meaning to what you gather. As always, your concept for this project should relate in some way to your larger Harmonic Collection theme.

Your collage should include:

  • At least 8 images and at least 3 words that connect to your Harmonic Collection theme. Treat your selection as intentional curation: what does it mean to bring these pieces together in one space?
  • A layout that responds to interaction: on hover, the design should shift or reveal something new. Think of this change as a way of activating your archive, allowing hidden relationships or layers to emerge.

Due 10/7

  1. In figma: Submit a link to a Figma design for your collage. Your design must include a clear layout guide using columns, a uniform grid, or rows. Consider how these structures echo archival systems of organization: grids, indexes, and categories that shape how we experience collections. Submitting a figma design is mandatory.
  2. In HTML & CSS code: Using HTML & CSS code your design into a functional website. Submit all of your project files (image assets, html, and CSS) here
