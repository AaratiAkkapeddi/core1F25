Project 9: Interactive Storytelling/Sequence

Due 11/25 9am

For this assignment, you'll create a multi-step interactive experience where users navigate through a narrative or journey related to your harmonic collection theme. Think of it as a "choose your own adventure" where user decisions determine what content appears next and what outcomes they reach.

Design a sequence of steps/scenes where users make choices that affect what they see. This could be building something step-by-step, making decisions that lead to different outcomes, or guiding users through a process. The key is thoughtful interaction design - consider how users move through your experience and why each choice matters.

Technical Requirements:

  • At least 3 different types of inputs (buttons, dropdowns, radio buttons, checkboxes, color pickers, sliders, text inputs, etc.)
  • At least 3 different visual changes that occur based on user interaction, you can use:
    • getElementById to target elements
    • .classList.add(), .classList.remove(), or .classList.toggle() to show/hide or style elements
    • .innerHTML to update text content
    • .src to change images
  • Multiple steps/scenes/states (minimum 3-4 distinct stages)
  • Responsive design that works on both desktop and mobile

Grading Criteria:

  • Conceptual depth (30%): Clear, intentional perspective shift that engages with device context
  • Design execution (30%): Both versions are polished, cohesive, and well-designed
  • Technical implementation (30%): Clean code, effective use of media queries, JS, semantic HTML
  • Integration with portfolio theme (10%): Successfully connects to your larger body of work

Example ideas:

  • Design a room step-by-step
  • Plan a night out with branching choices
  • Choreograph a dance routine
  • Guide someone through making the perfect recipe
