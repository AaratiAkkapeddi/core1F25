Begin creating a style guide for your harmonic collection in figma

Example: Small Editions Styleguide

Suggestions:

  • Typography -- what fonts, type sizes, and treatments are you using across your harmonic collection?
  • Button treatments -- what do your buttons look like on hover and before being hovered (corner radius, border?, text color, background color, etc)
  • Link treatment -- what do links look like before and after hovering?
  • Footer -- what does the footer look like on desktop vs mobile
  • Navigation -- what does the nav look like on desktop vs mobile
  • Color scheme -- what is the color pallette of your harmonic collection?
